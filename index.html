Our Achievements

  • Deployment of 125 Ex-servicemen. Approached Zila Sainik Board of Haryana, Rajasthan, Delhi and UP. These organization could only muster 43 ex-servicemen. Company worked day and night approaching individuals from Regimental Centers and Junior Commissioned officers. Successfully garnered the strength.

    DMRC

  • Rampage, looting, lynching, murder incident in 2012. Frantic communication received from Chief Security Officer regarding immediate armed manpower for mob control, personal protection and asset protection, as Haryana Police and G4 had declined to cooperate. In 24 hours 75 PSOs and armed guards were mobilized. MD and team personally present during deployment, guiding the personnel and assessing situation along with security team of Suzuki. Situation was controlled.

    Suzuki Motors India Ltd

  • Company approached by Army Welfare Placement Organization for immediate meeting with Vice President Arcil. During briefing, told about rampant pilferage, theft and sabotage of Thermal Plant at Chandwa (Jharkhand). Same day, Officer flown to the site for carrying out Threat Assessment and Security Audit. Written report submitted. Requirement for armed ex-servicemen suggested. Personnel mobilized within 4 days. Within 3 days of placement of personnel carrying out patrolling of 480 arces of area, laying ambush and spot checking within the perimeter, no further incidents have been reported. Aggregated loss to the Company before we took over, was approximately 600 Crores.

    Asset Reconstruction Company India Ltd. (Arcil)

  • Work order received just one day prior to deployment of 9 Supervisors and 97 guards. Cyclops team worked through out the night and were able to deploy all individuals.

    Department of Social Welfare
