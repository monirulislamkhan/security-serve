Our Profile

  • Registered under the Companies Act, 1956 as Pvt. Ltd. Company.
  • ISO 9001 – 2008 ; 14001:2004 ; OHSAS 18001:2007 compliant.
  • Registered and Certified with Private Security Agencies Regulation Act (PSARA), 2005, for the states of Delhi, Haryana, Rajasthan, UP, MP and Uttra Khand.
  • Managerial Board of experienced Subject Matter Experts in varied domains.
  • Competency gained in PSUs, Quasi Govt. Units, Hotels, Industrial Plants, Private Enterprises, Banking Sector etal.
  • Corpus of highly skilled, trained and dedicated Ex Servicemen and private security personnel.
  • Dedicated affiliation with Skill Development Institutes (Rajasthan & Delhi).
